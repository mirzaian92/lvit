## What Las Vegas businesses usually need first

Most environments have a mix of cloud apps and legacy dependencies, so we start by documenting what actually runs the business.

## Where onsite work changes the outcome

Remote support solves a lot, but Las Vegas has plenty of situations where onsite is faster and safer:

- Hardware swaps and encrypted device recovery that require custody control
- Wi-Fi and cabling issues that show up in physical spaces
- Shared workstations at front desks and service counters

We plan onsite access and timing around streets like Las Vegas Boulevard, Sahara Avenue, Charleston Boulevard, and Flamingo Road, with primary routes using I-15 and US 95 depending on where you are in the valley.
