## Onsite entry and parking is part of the support plan

Paradise locations often have tighter access rules than a typical office suite. If a site requires security desk check-in, loading dock access, or specific vendor parking, we prefer to document it. That sounds small, but it reduces response delays when a network device fails or a front-desk workstation needs a fast swap. The goal is simple: onsite work should be a planned execution, not a logistics surprise.
