## Mesquite note: plan around single points of failure

Many smaller environments rely on a single internet circuit, a single router, and a small number of critical devices. When one component fails, it can feel like the entire business is down. We reduce that risk by documenting dependencies, defining what is truly critical, and planning realistic failover steps where they are practical. Even a simple plan, like keeping a spare router pre-configured, can reduce downtime significantly.
