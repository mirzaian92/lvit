## A common Lone Mountain scenario: small teams with big dependencies

We frequently see small offices that rely on a short list of critical tools: email, file access, line-of-business apps, and phones. When one of those fails, it can stop the whole day. Our approach is to document the dependencies, define a basic recovery path, and standardize devices so a replacement is not a rebuild. That keeps support fast and reduces the risk of one device becoming a single point of failure.
